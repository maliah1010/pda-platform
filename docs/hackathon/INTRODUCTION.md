# The Introduction: Ant Newman & OPAL

## The Bio (use everywhere — LinkedIn, newsletter, conference, press)

**Short (1 line):**
Applied mathematician. 20 years deploying AI. Built OPAL — the open-source assurance framework for project delivery.

**Medium (3 lines):**
Ant Newman is an applied mathematician and CEO of TortoiseAI. Over 20 years he has built and deployed AI systems across industries. He created OPAL (Open Project Assurance Library), ARMM (Agent Readiness Maturity Model), and UDS (Universal Dashboard Specification) — the open-source stack for AI-native project assurance. His research paper *From Policy to Practice* is published with DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18711384.

**Full (conference/press):**
Ant Newman is an applied mathematician who has spent over two decades building and deploying AI in production environments. As CEO and Co-Founder of TortoiseAI, he created the PDA Platform — the first open-source, AI-native project assurance system for government and enterprise. The platform includes OPAL (Open Project Assurance Library), a 12-module assurance framework; ARMM (Agent Readiness Maturity Model), a 251-criteria maturity assessment; and UDS (Universal Dashboard Specification), an open standard for analytical dashboards. The platform is available as a live MCP server that any AI can connect to in 30 seconds, with 29 tools spanning compliance tracking, assumption drift analysis, domain classification, GMPP reporting, and AI-powered narrative generation. His work is backed by the published paper *From Policy to Practice: An Open Framework for AI-Ready Project Delivery* (Newman, 2026).
